Nine Inch Nails - Lights In The Sky Tour


Nine Inch Nails played at the Scottrade Center on 08.20.2008. Easily the best show I have ever seen. Upon arriving we were told the section our tickets were located in(10 rows deep into the top level, extreme left side of the stage) had been closed and we recieved new tickets. When we got to our new seats we found ourselves pretty much right in the middle of the stage, 18 rows from the floor. The night only got better. There is no way to describe the multimedia innovation that was the show so I took pics. I'm hoping Trent will produce a DVD similar to "And All That Could Have Been" for a more complete memorandum of last night's event.
