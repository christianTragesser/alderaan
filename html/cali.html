My trip to California


So this was my third trip this summer. My cousin Tim got married in Monterey, CA and since this was my first trip out to California I wanted to see as much of northern California as I could. We flew into San Jose and made the trek up to San Francisco and then toured certain locations through out Silicon Valley. Next we traveled through a forest of Redwoods and made the trip to Monterey via California HWY 1. California is everything(climate and country wise) that you hear about in media. My only regret is that I didnt have more time in these areas. I broke the pics up into 3 different pages to save on load time.
